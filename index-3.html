Abacus and Vedic Maths Center

ABACUS

  • An abacus is a manual tool used to keep track of numbers and to perform basic mathematical operations.
  • In addition to calculating the basic functions of addition, subtraction, multiplication and division, the abacus can calculate roots up to the cubic degree.
  • To use abacus tool kids use their fingers, using fingers gives them an opportunity to activate the brain sensors.
  • Here, at TreeTop we conduct Abacus coaching to help the young kids!

VEDIC MATHS

  • Vedic Mathematics is a collection of Techniques to solve mathematical arithmetics in easy and faster way.
  • it has Formulae which can be used for problems involved in arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus, conics.
  • At TreeTop we want kids to learn different approach of solving.
