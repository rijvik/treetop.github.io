Our Classes

PLAY GROUP


Age Group: 1.5 - 2.5 Years
The objective of the program is to train parents to help them foster their child's development by learning and practising child development through a scientific approach.
Everyday is filled with fun activities and everything we do is designed to educate.

NURSERY


Age Group: 2.5 - 3.5 Years
In Nursery, children are engaged in school readiness activities such as reading, writing, counting, number recognition and problem solving in a fun filled but purposeful manner.
The nursery curriculum provides a right array of activities to help children move towards achieving their early learning goals.
